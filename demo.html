Understanding the Simulation

  • Red particles represent positive charges
  • Blue particles represent negative charges
  • Arrows show the direction and magnitude of the force
  • Opposite charges attract (force pulls them together)
  • Like charges repel (force pushes them apart)
  • Force increases as charges get closer (inverse square law)
